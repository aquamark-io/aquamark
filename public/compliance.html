Commercial Financing Compliance

Broker and lender requirements by state

California

  • Brokers: Must hold a California Financing Law (CFL) license; disclosures required.
  • Lenders: Must disclose total repayment amount, APR, and terms for deals ≤ $500,000.

Connecticut

  • Brokers: Must register; required to provide disclosures for sales-based financing ≤ $250,000.
  • Lenders: Must disclose financing amount, fees, terms, and broker compensation.

Georgia

  • Brokers: No active broker requirements.
  • Lenders: Must provide disclosures for sales-based financing ≤ $500,000.

New York

  • Brokers: Must provide disclosure form prior to presenting offers.
  • Lenders: Required to disclose APR, total cost, term, and fees for deals ≤ $2.5 million.

Utah

  • Brokers: Must register with the state; disclosures required.
  • Lenders: Must disclose financing terms and broker compensation for deals ≤ $1 million.

Virginia

  • Brokers: Must register; required to issue disclosures for sales-based financing.
  • Lenders: Must disclose financing terms, fees, and broker compensation.
