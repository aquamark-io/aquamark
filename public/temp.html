Backdooring Impacts Us All

Backdooring is one of the biggest threats in the MCA industry. It happens when another broker or lender steals the originating brokers files.

The result is broken trust across the board, lost commissions, wasted resources for everyone, and merchants who either walk away or get trapped in stacking cycles that put their businesses at risk.

In the end, it drags down everyone—and fuels the perception that our industry can't be trusted.
